How to Reverse Seer a Steak

~Ingredients~

  • 1 thick cut steak (personal choice is a Ribeye)
  • 6 grams of Course Sea Salt
  • 4 Whole Pepper corns
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 2 Thyme Branches
  • 5 Cubes of Butter

~Utensils~

    A Oven
    A Pan
    A Cooking Thermometer
    A Wire Rack
    1 Tablespoon
    A Motar & Pestle
    Aluminum Foil

~Method~

  1. Roast the Pepper Corns on the pan until popping of the Pepper
  2. Use the Motar & Pestle to crush the Pepper, this will bring out a lot more flavor and armoa outta the Pepper
  3. Season the Steak with Salt & Pepper
  4. Place the Steak on the Wire Rack
  5. Preheat the Oven to 135c/275f
  6. Place the Steak inside the Oven and cook until an internal temp of 115f which usaully takes 50 minutes, but depends on the thickness of the Steak
  7. Preheat the pan to high
  8. Sear the Steak for 1 minute on 1 side and then flip
  9. Move the Steak to the top of the pan away from you
  10. Add the Thyme, Garlic and Butter
  11. Tilt the pan up and using the Tablespoon start to baste (which is to scoop up the melting butter along with all flavors in the pan with the spoon and dump it over the meat, in succession)
  12. Once the 1-1Â½ minutes are up serve the meat (Don't let it rest this time)
  13. Enjoy!
